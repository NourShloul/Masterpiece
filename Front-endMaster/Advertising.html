Frequently Asked Questions

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is an online marketing model where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. It is a way to buy visits to your site, rather than attempting to earn those visits organically.

Advertisers bid on keywords that they believe their target market will use as search terms. Ads are then placed on search engine results pages or other websites, and the advertiser pays a fee each time someone clicks on their ad.

PPC advertising can drive immediate traffic to your website, improve brand visibility, and generate leads and sales quickly. It is highly measurable and allows for precise targeting, making it an effective tool for online marketing.
